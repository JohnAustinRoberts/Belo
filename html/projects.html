The Tank!

This is a log of the progress we are making in the construction of our paintball tank. We are using a 1994 Ford F-150 as the base, and plan on creating a pvc and netting cage to fit over the bed of the truck. We are going to be hand-crafting our main cannon using pvc to create a breach loading pnuematic cannon. We also plan on creating two smaller pneumatic cannons as spaldings.
